How to flash

  1. Plug your XIAO ESP32-C6 into USB. Use a data cable, not a charge-only cable.
  2. Enter bootloader mode: hold the BOOT button, tap RESET, then release BOOT. The LED will stay off.
  3. Click Install above and select the COM / ttyUSB port that appears.
  4. Wait ~60 seconds for the flash to complete. Do not unplug during flashing.
  5. Pair with Zigbee2MQTT: enable Permit Join in Z2M, then tap RESET once on your XIAO. The device will appear as HiveKit SCD40.
  6. Add the Z2M converter if not auto-discovered: copy hivekit-scd40.js to /config/zigbee2mqtt/external_converters/ and restart Z2M.

Trouble flashing? Make sure no other app (Arduino IDE, ESPHome) has the serial port open. On Windows, you may need the CP210x USB driver.
